Big Bay State Park

Park Information


Located on Madeline Island in Northern Wisconsin. Big Bay State Park has a 1.5-mile beach, a familiy campground and outdoor group camp, picnic areas, a 1.3 mile boardwalk and more than 7 miles of hiking and nature trails with incredible views of Lake Superior.

The park is open year-round from 6:00am to 11:00pm. To access the park you must ride the Madeline Island Ferry Boat. The boat operates on regular schedules between Bayfield and La Pointe from April to December.

Kayak along Wisconsin's only state park on beautiful Lake Superior, with some freshwater sea caves to the west and one of the Midwest's oceans to the east. You can bring your own kayak or rent one at Barrier Beach.

Paddle around the sandstone cliffs through sea caves and turquoise waters. Click here to watch the adventures of kayaking at Big Bay State Park: Kayaking Video.
